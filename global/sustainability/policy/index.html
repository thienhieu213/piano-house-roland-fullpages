Mission

THRILL AND EXCITEMENT
Waku Waku: The Thrill and Excitement of Creative Experiences

Roland's mission is not only to produce simple-yet-powerful tools for creativity —it's to inspire people around the world with the thrill and excitement of creative experiences that surprise, delight, and stir the soul.
Roland's desire as a company is to be equally excited by the music, videos, and other content people create using our products.
In Japan, “waku waku” is the expression for this kind of thrill and excitement, and Roland promises to share lots of “waku waku” in the future.

Importance of Realizing the Sustainability

The Roland business contributes to the sustainable development of the society through its musical and video culture while, at the same time, it is supported by the stability and affluence of the environment and the entire society. In addition, we are aware that it is an important duty that a corporation should assume to confront in earnest such issues as those related to climate change or human rights and to contribute to the solution of those.

Aiming at avoiding the occurrence of the negative chain reaction in which the stability or sustainability of the environment or of the society is damaged causing the musical and video cultures or our company's business not to be able to exist any longer, we are taking actions as our managerial materiality in order to create a virtuous cycle where each one heightens the other one's sustainability.

Policy for the Initiatives

In accordance with the following principles for our activities, we will continue providing the value of “Creativity” for development of the musical and video cultures, and do our “BEST” for balancing the solution of environmental and social issues and the growth of our business, through the initiatives with “Cooperative Enthusiasm for All Stakeholders”.

The five principles for activities

Supervising System

With regard to initiatives concerning our company's sustainability, we define “Importance of Realizing the Sustainability,” “Policy for the Initiatives” and “Supervision System” as our “Basic Sustainability Policy”, and we approve them after deliberating over those with the view of medium-and long- term increase in corporate value, including specifying the “Materiality”, at the Board of Directors meeting of Roland Corporation.
In activities, the Board of Directors receives regular reports and performs [Supervision] on ensuring [Awareness], [Practice], [Disclosure] and the premise [Attitude] of the above “principles for activities”, and also provide advice and support if necessary.

Materiality

As we consider that realizing the sustainability should be to “respond to the expectations of all the stakeholders,” we have summarized the initiatives to be focused from the aspects of risks and opportunities for our business, and specified the following materiality.

Materiality Theme of initiative

Reinforcement of supply chain Management.
  • Improvement of supply chain efficiency.
  • Stabilization of product supply system.
  • Reduction of the number of models
  • Expanding the use of renewable energy
  • Strengthening our relationship with suppliers and dealers.
  • Human rights protection in the supply chain.
  • Reduction of environmental burdens from the domain of product designing.
  • Understanding the situation of CO2 emissions and examining reduction measures

Support of the development of musical and video cultures.
  • Provision of platforms for activities.
  • Support of artists and the industry.
  • Launch of game-changer products.
  • Social contribution in the musical/video fields and neighboring fields.
    (Support for disabled people, music therapy, musical education, etc.)

Maximization of opportunities for human resources to display their vitality and capacity.
  • Promotion of females' active participation in business.
  • Promotion of diversified workstyles.
  • Maintenance of validity of incentives.
  • Reinforcement of the global personnel management.

Investment for the growth (in intangible assets).
  • Promotion of the Roland Cloud business.
    (Promotion of development of software, contents, etc.)
  • Provision of the greatest customer satisfaction and customer experiences.
    (Utilization of the digital technology for our marketing and support)
  • Utilization of data for the optimum supply of products.

Unrelenting reinforcement of the corporate governance.

At the same time, it affects the overall initiatives.
  • Further increase of the effectiveness of corporate governance.
  • Further fulfilling information disclosure.
  • Strengthening of risk management and compliance.
  • Supervision by the Board of Directors on our initiatives for sustainability.
