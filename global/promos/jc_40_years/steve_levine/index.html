Artist Bio

Steve Levine is a producer of the highest caliber, with credits including such bands as The Clash, The Beach Boys, and Culture Club. Steve has a long history with Roland and BOSS, going back to the mid-1970s when he began his career as a tape-op at CBS Studios. After highly acclaimed reviews of his production on Beach Boys and Culture Club records, he found himself hitting the desk for Motorhead, Ziggy Marley, and Gary Moore. Steve is now chairman of the Music Producers Guild, and teaches masterclasses for Apple on production.
