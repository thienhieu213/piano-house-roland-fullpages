R-07 Guide

R-07 High-Resolution
Audio
Recorder
PRACTICAL
GUIDE

Capture the moment in high resolution with the R-07.

You never know where you’ll be when creative inspiration strikes or you hear something you don’t want to miss. With the R-07 in your pocket, backpack, or bag, you’re always ready to capture high-quality recordings at a moment’s notice. Offering an all-in-one design with simple controls, the R-07 provides everything you need in a small, robust device that you can take everywhere.
