The power and flexibility of the ZEN-Core Synthesis System delivers an incredible array of sounds, from analog modelling synths to crystal-clear pianos. Whether you want to keep your music fresh with genre-focused Sound Packs, or you’d prefer to explore a specific instrument via the Wave Expansion series, ZEN-Core is the gateway to a constantly evolving collection of sounds, created by some of the world’s most exciting sound designers.

ZEN-Core
Sound Packs

ZEN-Core Sound Packs are genre-specific sound collections that give you an extra shot of inspiration—complete with deep editing and programming options absent from most sound packs. ZEN-Core sound packs will be dropping regularly too, so your music will always sound fresh.

Explore Sound Packs



ZEN-Core
Wave Expansions

This fifteen-strong library contains thousands of tried-and-tested sounds that are synonymous with modern music. From pianos and drums, to vintage synths and dance music, there’s enough material to keep you making music for years to come.

Available for ZENOLOGY, FANTOM and JUPITER-X series.

Explore Wave Expansions

Instrument-specific Expansions

Starting with JUPITER-X and FANTOM, ZEN-Core offers hardware-based, instrument-specific expansions, each delivering unparalleled depth and clarity. From analog and piano modelling, to PCM waveforms and beyond, ZEN-Core brings the sounds you need to life.

Explore ZEN-Core hardware

Share sounds across hardware and software

Take collaboration to the next level and share a sound with bandmates or co-creator—even if you all own different Roland hardware. With ZEN-Core you get the sounds you want, in the format you want, wherever you want.
