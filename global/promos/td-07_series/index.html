TD-07 SERIES CONCEPT

Sound, sensitivity and stability:
all as standard

The TD-07 series supports every drummer as they take the next step, offering four different kits with unique configurations. But whichever TD-07 kit you choose, you always get these core elements.

TD-07 module full of authentic drum sounds and wireless connectivity

+

Dual mesh heads that capture the nuance of your playing

+

Sturdy drum stand and hardware clamps that are built to last

AMAZING DRUM SOUNDS

TD-07 module

When you just want to play drums, the TD-07 sound module gets you in the zone, putting authentic pro-drum tones at your fingertips and letting you focus on the sticks, not the dials. But when you want to drill deeper, this powerful module guides you through its features without breaking your flow. You can even edit the presets to make your own signature custom kits.

REALISTIC PLAYING FEEL

Dual-mesh pads

Play Roland’s dual-mesh heads and you’ll never settle for another digital pad. With two layers of a unique material designed for optimum response, you’ll feel the difference from your first hit. Stick rebound and resistance feel just like a traditional acoustic kit. Unbeatable strike-detection makes for reliable sound triggering every time, while ultra-quiet stick-to-pad contact means your drumming will never disturb others.

BUILT TO LAST

Roland drum stand

The TD-07 series stand is quick to set up and easy to place in your room thanks to its compact footprint. The strong and stable metal construction also lets you drum as long or hard as you like with complete confidence: Roland drum stands are still in service after years of continuous use. Plus, with the stand components specially designed to reduce vibration and noise transfer to the floor, the TD-07 kits are one of the quietest electronic drums you’ll ever play.

TD-07 MODULE DEEP DIVE

The compact drum module with big ideas

All kits in the TD-07 series are powered by the TD-07 module. It’s packed full of super-playable drums—from authentic acoustic to punchy electronic—and is designed so you can jump straight in and start playing. Although simple to use, there’s enough depth to ensure you won’t outgrow the drum kit as your playing develops.

CHOOSING THE RIGHT KIT FOR YOU

Four kits to suit the way you play

Now you know what the TD-07 series is about, pick the kit that suits your setup.
As you move up the range, the pads get bigger, you can add a second crash cymbal, or even upgrade to floating-design hi-hats. And the sound module, dual-mesh drum heads, and road-tested drum stand are standard across the whole series, so just pick the configuration that appeals and start drumming.


TD-07DMK

The entry-level TD-07DMK is the most affordable kit in the TD-07 series, with a compact pad layout and a kick pad that attaches directly to the stand.

Perfect if you need a drum kit that can fit into smaller spaces, with compact pad size and configuration.

The slimline kick pad attaches directly to the drum stand, taking up hardly any room and is ideal if the kit is frequently moved around or needs to be stored away after use.

What do you get with this kit?

  • Module: TD-07
  • Snare: PDX-8, 10-inch
  • Tom1: PDX-6A, 8-inch
  • Tom2: PDX-6A, 8-inch
  • Tom3: PDX-6A, 8-inch
  • Hi-hat: CY-5, 10-inch
  • Crash: CY-5, 10-inch
  • Ride: CY-5, 10-inch
  • Kick: Pad attaches to stand
  • Stand: Four-post stand

TD-07KV

The TD-07KV is great value within the TD-07 family, with an upgraded freestanding kick drum, and bigger crash cymbals.

While offering the same compact stand size as the entry-level TD-07DMK, this uprated kit benefits from the added expression and control of a larger individual KD-10 kick pad and CY-8 crash cymbal.

The larger and more substantial kick pad means your leg gets a great work out, helping developing stamina and technique across a range of musical styles.

Crash and ride cymbals are bigger giving you a larger target to hit, along with more movement and swing when struck.

What’s upgraded?

Same compact stand size, but with a freestanding kick pad and larger 12-inch crash and ride cymbals

What do you get with this kit?

  • Module: TD-07
  • Snare: PDX-8, 10-inch
  • Tom1: PDX-6A, 8-inch
  • Tom2: PDX-6A, 8-inch
  • Tom3: PDX-6A, 8-inch
  • Hi-hat: CY-5, 10-inch
  • Crash: CY-8, 12-inch
  • Ride: CY-8, 12-inch
  • Kick: KD-10 kick pad
  • Stand: Four-post stand

TD-07KX

The TD-07KX has the most cymbals in the TD-07 lineup thanks to an extra crash, with bigger snare and toms too.

If you have the space and budget, larger drum pads are better for freedom of movement, so choose the TD-07KX for the next step up.

This mid-series kit offers larger snare and toms, giving more space for drum rudiments, fills, and classic drum solos.

The additional crash cymbal introduces more opportunities for playing extra sounds while also developing your co-ordination skills and stamina.

What’s upgraded?

Larger 12-inch snare and 8-inch toms, and a second crash cymbal (12 inch)

What do you get with this kit?

  • Module: TD-07
  • Snare: PDX-12, 12-inch
  • Tom1: PDX-8, 10-inch
  • Tom2: PDX-8, 10-inch
  • Tom3: PDX-8, 10-inch
  • Hi-hat: CY-5, 10-inch
  • Crash: CY-8, 12-inch
  • Crash 2: CY-8, 12-inch
  • Ride: CY-8, 12-inch
  • Kick: KD-10 kick pad
  • Stand: Four-post stand

VIEW KIT

TD-07KVX

The TD-07KVX is top of the TD-07 range, with a floating VH-10 hi-hat that mounts onto an acoustic hi-hat stand, plus larger V-Cymbals.

Play your most dynamic and dramatic parts with a full array of cymbals, including the freestanding VH-10 hi-hats for a more substantial physical presence and superior playability.

The enlarged crash and ride V-Cymbals give you an even larger target to hit, along with satisfying movement and swing when struck.

The three-zone ride means you can play the bow, bell, and edge of the cymbal for maximum expression.

What’s upgraded?

VH-10 floating hi-hats mounted on its own stand, larger cymbals (12 and 13 inch) with three-zone design for the ride cymbal

What do you get with this kit?

  • Module: TD-07
  • Snare: PDX-12, 12-inch
  • Tom1: PDX-8, 10-inch
  • Tom2: PDX-8, 10-inch
  • Tom3: PDX-8, 10-inch
  • Hi-hat: VH-10, floating hi-hat
  • Crash: CY-12C, 12-inch
  • Crash 2: CY-13R-BK, 13-inch
  • Ride: CY-8, 12-inch
  • Kick: KD-10 kick pad
  • Stand: Four-post stand
