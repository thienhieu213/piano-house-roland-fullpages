ROLAND SERATO TR-SYNC

The Serato x Roland TR-SYNC update brings the power of Roland’s iconic drum machines to your DJ setup. Simply connect your Roland TR drum machine to Serato DJ Pro and they’ll sync to the same BPM. Now you can easily add your own drum loops with the same drum sounds used on your favorite music. Flip genres, beef up classic tracks, remix and transition between songs in new ways. Anyone can play the next big track, but how many DJs can make it their own?
Hook up your Roland TR drum machine to Serato DJ Pro and elevate your game.


SERATO TR-SYNC VIDEOS

The selection of Serato TR-SYNC videos below shows you the true potential of combining a Roland drum machine to your Serato DJ setup to bring exciting new techniques to your live set.

TR-SYNC Set Up

Learn how to SYNC your TR drum machine with Serato DJ Pro. This video lets you know what Roland TR drum machines are supported, how to connect to your laptop, audio routing, and a quick overview of beatgridding.

TR-SYNC Techniques and Tips for DJs

A deeper dive into how you can use your TR-SYNC set up to add drums on top of your DJ set, remix live, create unique transitions, beef up classic tracks, expanding your DJ set up, as well as programming basics on a Roland TR drum machine.

Miles Medina TR-SYNC Routine

Miles Medina, 2017 Goldie Award winner and DJ hardware master, crafted a classic Serato TR-SYNC routine using the Roland TR-8S integration with Serato DJ Pro.

BPM Supreme TR-SYNC ‘READY’ Tracks

Matthew Chicoine, a.k.a. Recloose, shows you how to get started using BPM Supreme TR-READY tracks alongside Serato DJ Pro and Roland’s iconic TR-8, TR-8S, TR-08, and TR-09 drum machines.
