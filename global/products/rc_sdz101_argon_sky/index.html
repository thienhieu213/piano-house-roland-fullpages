SDZ101 Argon Sky

Sound Pack

Join Now

Retro Analog and Digital Sounds for ZEN-Core Instruments

Envelope your tracks in retro atmospherics with Argon Sky—an arresting assemblage of analog and digital textures for synthwave, ’80s pop, and mainstream music styles. Compatible with the ZENOLOGY Software Synthesizer and Roland hardware instruments with the ZEN-Core Synthesis System, this curated collection features 32 expertly crafted tones and two drum kits.

  • ZEN-Core sounds for synthwave, retro pop, and mainstream styles
  • Includes 32 tones and two drum kits
  • Available for download in Roland Cloud Manager
  • Compatible with the ZENOLOGY Software Synthesizer and Roland ZEN-Core hardware instruments

SDZ101 Argon Sky Sound Examples
Expand Your Creative Options with ZEN-Core Sound Packs

ZEN-Core Sound Packs provide new sounds for Roland’s ZENOLOGY Software Synthesizer and ZEN-Core hardware. Driven by the powerful ZEN-Core Synthesis System, they deliver everything from vintage analog to fresh hybrid tones. Each pack focuses on a specific genre or instrument type, or features sounds from leading artists.

How Do I Get ZEN-Core Sound Packs?

ZEN-Core Sound Packs are available for download in Roland Cloud Manager.

Will ZEN-Core Sound Packs Work with My Roland Hardware Instrument?

Visit the Support tab for a complete list of compatible Roland ZEN-Core instruments.
