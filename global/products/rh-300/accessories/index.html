Premium Closed-Back Studio Headphones for Pro Level Monitoring

When you’re working in a professional studio with the best equipment and musicians, a great set of headphones can make all the difference. Roland’s premium RH-300 monitor headphones are specially designed to offer unbeatable monitoring and supreme comfort to players, producers and engineers working at the highest level. Featuring custom-built 45mm drivers, neodymium magnets and CCAW (copper-clad aluminum wire) voice coils, expect the clearest, most natural and accurate sound, plus ultra-wide frequency response that brings out sparkling highs and warm lows. For ambitious music-makers, RH-300 headphones are the essential choice.

Standard Features

  • Roland’s top-of-the-range closed-back monitor headphones for the best monitoring experience
  • Optimized for monitoring any musical instrument, with natural, flat and accurate sound
  • Custom 45mm drivers with neodymium magnets provide an ultra-wide frequency range for higher sonic authenticity
  • Robust build quality and stylish aluminum housing plate
  • Lightweight design with soft earpads for comfort

PREMIUM MONITORING HEADPHONES, FOR MODERN PROFESSIONALS

To create a successful track, you need to hear every element of the mix in minute detail. Make Roland RH-300 headphones a mainstay of your studio setup and you’ll always be in control. Whatever instrument you’re monitoring – from synths to electronic drums, or even a full-band mix – the custom 45mm drivers with neodymium magnets offer neutral and detailed sound, plus an ultra-wide frequency range from treble to bass. Choose RH-300 headphones to hear the entire soundscape, tailor it for your audience – and avoid the ear fatigue that creeps in with other sets.

LONG SESSIONS RUN SMOOTH, WITH SUPERIOR COMFORT AND NOISE ISOLATION

Whether you’re studying a mix or tracking a vocal, it’s essential to choose headphones that prevent sound leakage. With its closed-back design, the RH-300 offer professional noise isolation in both directions, stopping external noise getting into the cans, or a backing track bleeding into the microphone while you’re singing. Better still, all that tech won’t slow you down. Sleek and stylish, with a striking aluminum housing plate, RH-300 headphones score with their lightweight construction and soft earpads: even during demanding all-night sessions, you’ll almost forget you’re wearing them.
