KD-A22

Kick Drum Converter

Convert Your 22-Inch Acoustic Kick Drum into a Trigger for V-Drums Modules

If you love the look and feel of your traditional acoustic bass drum, the Roland KD-A22 Kick Drum Converter lets you incorporate it into your V-Drums setup. This new trigger package fits to a standard 22-inch acoustic bass drum, giving you striking stage presence and acoustic-style feel thanks to Roland’s innovative air-damping mechanics working alongside the deep drum shell. The package includes everything you need to convert your 22-inch bass drum*—a front head, a batter head with a kick-pad sensor unit, a 22-inch metal hoop, and shock-absorbing cushions. The KD-A22 provides stable, consistent, and accurate triggering for your setup, and is fully compatible with double kick pedals.

* The KD-A22 package does not include a 22-inch bass drum or kick drum pedal.
* The product design is subject to change without notice, and may vary from the images shown here.

  • Compatible with a standard 22-inch acoustic bass drum
  • Stable and accurate triggering for single- and double-pedal kick work
  • Strong visual presence on the stage
