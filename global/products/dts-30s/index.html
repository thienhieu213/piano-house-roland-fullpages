DTS-30S

Stand Set

Premium Heavy-Duty Stand and Cable Set for V-Drums

Developed for the flagship VAD706 kit in the V-Drums Acoustic Design series, the DTS-30S stand set is also compatible with other V-Drums kits and components. It comes with two DCS-30 cymbal/tom stands, one DBS-30 cymbal boom stand, and one PDS-20 module stand. Also included is a complete set of TRS trigger cables with transparent jackets for a discrete look.

Designed for Roland V-Cymbals, the DCS-30 and DBS-30 feature cymbal mounts with the appropriate clearance for natural swinging motion. Three telescopic tube sections let you set the most comfortable height for your layout, and the boom arm has a ball clamp mount for flexible cymbal positioning. The L-rod tom mounts on the DCS-30 stands are perfect for Roland V-Pads, with strong ball clamps that can be adjusted without affecting the cymbal height. Completing the set is the PDS-20, which provides a stable foundation for a V-Drums sound module or a Roland percussion instrument from the SPD and HandSonic series. All stands feature double-braced tripod legs and large diameter tubes for strength and stability.

  • Heavy-duty stand set with two DCS-30 combination stands, one DBS-30 cymbal boom stand, and one PDS-20 module/pad stand
  • Rugged chrome plating
  • Double-braced tripod legs with sturdy rubber feet for stability
  • Large diameter pipes for extra strength and durability
  • DCS-30 and DBS-30 have telescopic boom arms with ball clamp mounts for flexible cymbal positioning
  • Create a straight stand with the DCS-30 and DBS-30 by nesting the boom arm in the top down tube
  • DCS-30 features an L-rod tom mount with ball clamp that can be adjusted independently
  • Also includes 11 TRS trigger connection cables with ties
