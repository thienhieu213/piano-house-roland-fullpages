Blues Cube Tour Amp Cover

This authentic Roland amp cover will help keep your Blues Cube Tour head looking new. Shields against dust, dirt, and grime while protecting from minor scuffs and scrapes. Made with durable polyester exterior and sized for a perfect fit.

  • Black color
  • Tan embroidered Blues Cube logo
  • Reinforced seams and handle opening
  • Interior/exterior: polyester
