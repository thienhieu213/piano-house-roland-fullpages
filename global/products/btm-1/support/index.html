Stream Your Favorite Music and Jam Along

If you love guitar and listening to music, Roland’s BTM-1 is the Bluetooth speaker you need. To start, it’s a high-quality mobile sound system for wirelessly streaming favorite songs from your smartphone or tablet. What’s more, it also has a guitar input to plug in your axe and jam along! Compact, convenient, and always ready to rock, the BTM-1 is your solution for reducing daily stress with some fun guitar riffing. And with its stylish looks, this attractive speaker fits right into your living space. Whether you used to play and want to reignite those skills or you’re a current player looking for an easy home practice solution, the versatile BTM-1 is your perfect daily companion.

Standard Features
View the Gallery

Jam with Friends and Family

The BTM-1 has an auxiliary input for connecting another device such as a synthesizer or digital wind instrument like Roland’s Aerophone series. This makes it easy and convenient to enjoy playing music along with a friend or family member. It’s also possible to stream backing music over Bluetooth while the aux input is used.

High-Performance Sound

The BTM-1’s advanced cabinet design and custom speakers are fully optimized for accurate audio reproduction from an amazingly small footprint. Backed by Roland’s decades of professional musical instrument development, the BTM-1 delivers an organic, well-balanced sound experience that’s perfectly tuned for both full-range music streaming and guitar playing.

Sleek, Stylish, and Ready to Play

The BTM-1 is streamlined and light, and its white and gold look blends nicely with modern home furnishings. It’s always ready to go when you want to play guitar, and you can even plug in headphones to silence the speaker for quiet playing when needed. Best of all, the BTM-1 does double-duty as a premium Bluetooth sound system that can be enjoyed by everyone in your home.

Enjoy Natural Guitar Tone and Feel

When you feel inspired to jam along with streamed music or simply practice alone, just connect your guitar to the BTM-1’s dedicated input and start rocking! The input provides natural touch and tone, while easy-access controls let you dial in sounds from clean to overdrive. There's even an echo effect to help your playing blend in seamlessly with your favorite music tracks.

Standard Features

  • Top-quality Bluetooth sound system for streaming music
  • Roland’s respected technology delivers clear, high-definition sound optimized for both guitar and full-range audio
  • Guitar input with inspiring guitar sound and feel with analog overdrive tone and rich echo effect
  • Stylish look blends nicely into modern living spaces
  • Compact, lightweight design and built-in rechargeable battery lets you play anytime and anywhere
  • Also includes an Aux input for playing another instrument or device along with Bluetooth audio
